Privacy

Here’s what happens with your data when you visit this site.

Nothing. This site doesn’t use analytics, tracking, cookies, or forms. You can browse freely without sharing any personal information.

Fonts

Typography uses the Inter font, served directly from this website (self-hosted font files). No font requests are sent to third parties for loading the typeface.

Links to other sites

You’ll find links to Apple Music, Spotify, Instagram, IMDb, and others. Nothing is sent to those services until you click. Once you leave this site, their privacy policies apply.

The contact link opens your email app. Nothing is sent to our servers when you click it. If you choose to email, you’re sharing your address and message voluntarily.

The company hosting this site may keep standard logs (IP address, time, pages visited) for security and maintenance. We don’t control or routinely access those logs.

Under GDPR and similar laws, you can ask to see, correct, or delete any data held about you. Since we don’t collect personal data here, there’s nothing to access or delete. If you have questions, reach out at c.lungala@icloud.com.
